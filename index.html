Pancake Recipe

Ingredients

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 2 cups milk
  • 2 eggs
  • 1/4 cup melted butter
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. Mix dry ingredients together.
  2. Whisk in wet ingredients.
  3. Cook on a heated pan until golden brown.
  4. Flip and cook the other side.
  5. Serve warm with syrup.
